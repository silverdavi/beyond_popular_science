% One-line biography: Itai Yanai
\textbf{Itai Yanai} (b. 1975). Israeli-American computational biologist and one of the pioneers of single-cell transcriptomics (CEL-Seq), co-author of \textit{The Society of Genes}, and founding director of the Institute for Computational Medicine at NYU. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:dnasequencing]{40}]}
