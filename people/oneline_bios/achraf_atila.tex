\textbf{Achraf Atila} (b. unknown). Computational materials scientist at the Bundesanstalt f\"{u}r Materialforschung und Pr\"{u}fung (BAM) in Berlin who co-authored molecular dynamics simulations demonstrating that cold displacement-driven amorphization dominates ice lubrication during sliding. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:iceslipperiness]{42}]}
