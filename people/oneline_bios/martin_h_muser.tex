% One-line biography: Martin H. M\"{u}ser
\textbf{Martin H. M\"{u}ser} (b. unknown). German computational physicist and professor at Saarland University who researches tribology and contact mechanics across scales, and co-authored simulations revealing cold displacement-driven amorphization as the dominant ice lubrication mechanism. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:iceslipperiness]{42}]}
