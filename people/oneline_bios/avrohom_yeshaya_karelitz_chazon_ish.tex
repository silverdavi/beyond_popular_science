% One-line biography: Avrohom Yeshaya Karelitz (Chazon Ish)
\textbf{Avrohom Yeshaya Karelitz (Chazon Ish)} (1878–1953). Haredi religious leader known as \emph{Chazon Ish} who codified agricultural laws in Israel and defined the halakhic international dateline. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:jewishcalendar]{26}]}
