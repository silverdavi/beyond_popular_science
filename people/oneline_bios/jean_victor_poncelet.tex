% One-line biography: Jean-Victor Poncelet
\textbf{Jean-Victor Poncelet} (1788–1867). French mathematician and engineer who founded modern projective geometry through his seminal work \emph{Trait\'{e} des propri\'{e}t\'{e}s projectives des figures}. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:billiardsconicsporism]{07}]}
