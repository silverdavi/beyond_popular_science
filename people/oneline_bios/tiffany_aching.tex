% One-line biography: Tiffany Aching
\textbf{Tiffany Aching} (Fictional). Young witch from Terry Pratchett's \emph{Discworld} series who protects the Chalk using practical wisdom and allies with the Nac Mac Feegle, embodying ``First Sight'' and ``Second Thoughts.'' \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:woodwardhoffmannrules]{46}]}
