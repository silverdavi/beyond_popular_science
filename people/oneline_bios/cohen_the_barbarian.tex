% One-line biography: Cohen the Barbarian
\textbf{Cohen the Barbarian} (Fictional). Elderly barbarian hero from Terry Pratchett's \emph{Discworld} who leads the Silver Horde, refuses to retire despite being over 90, and famously attempts to return fire to the gods. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:blackholetimedilationredshift]{23}]}
