% One-line biography: J. Michael Kosterlitz
\nobelphysics \textbf{J. Michael Kosterlitz} (b. 1943). British-American physicist who co-discovered the Kosterlitz-Thouless topological phase transition in two-dimensional systems, sharing the 2016 Nobel Prize. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:topologicalinsulators]{11}]}
