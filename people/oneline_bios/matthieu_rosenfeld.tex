% One-line biography: Matthieu Rosenfeld
\textbf{Matthieu Rosenfeld} (b. unknown). French mathematician and assistant professor at LIRMM, Universit\'{e} de Montpellier, who specializes in combinatorics on words and computer-assisted proofs, and proved the Lonely Runner Conjecture for eight and nine runners. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:arrowtheoremtopology]{09}]}
