% One-line biography: Tanupat Trakulthongchai
\textbf{Tanupat Trakulthongchai} (b. unknown). Mathematician who independently proved the Lonely Runner Conjecture for nine and ten runners in 2025, using a sieving method that refined Rosenfeld's computer-assisted backtracking approach. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:arrowtheoremtopology]{09}]}
