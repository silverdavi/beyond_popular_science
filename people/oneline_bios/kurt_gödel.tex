% One-line biography: Kurt Gödel
\textbf{Kurt Gödel} (1906–1978). Austrian-American logician and mathematician who proved the Incompleteness Theorems, establishing that consistent arithmetic systems must contain undecidable propositions. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:banach]{03}, \hyperref[ch:compacttwinparadox]{14}]}
