\textbf{Wit} (b. unknown). Ancient immortal worldhopper from Brandon Sanderson's \emph{Stormlight Archive} who serves as King's Wit on Roshar, wielding acerbic humor and cryptic wisdom while pursuing a millennia-spanning agenda. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:speculativeexecutionattacks]{18}]}
