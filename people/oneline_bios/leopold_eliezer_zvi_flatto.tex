% One-line biography: Leopold Eliezer Zvi Flatto
\textbf{Leopold Eliezer Zvi Flatto} (b. unknown). American mathematician who authored the definitive monograph \emph{Poncelet's Theorem} exploring connections to elliptic curves and billiard dynamics. \emph{[Ch.~\hyperref[ch:billiardsconicsporism]{07}]}
